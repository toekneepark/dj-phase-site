DJ Phase isn't just a DJ — he's a full-spectrum music personality. By day, he's an on-air voice on iHeartRadio, connecting with listeners across the Pacific Northwest. By night (and weekends), he's behind the decks at Seattle's hottest venues, turning every room into a party.

From rocking the 300 Level concourse at Seahawks games in Lumen Field to holding down weekly residencies at Fuego Bar and Legion Sports Bar, DJ Phase has built a reputation as Seattle's most versatile DJ. His secret? He reads the room like nobody else — seamlessly blending house, R&B, top 40, remixes, and mashups to keep every crowd moving.

He's also the creator of [Bar Bangerz](https://www.barbargerz.com) — a curated collection of custom DJ edits, remixes, and mashups used by DJs everywhere. When he's not on air or on stage, you'll find him creating vlogs, connecting with fans, and living music 24/7.
